How to prevent from COVID-19?

What to do?

Wash your hand for 20 sec.

Please have a habit of washing of hands for 20 sec atleast to kill germs and bacteria. Use hand-sanitizers.

Wear mask all the time.

Wear quality mask for 24/7 to avoid spreading of covid-19. Avoid quality less masks.

Stay home

Don't go outside of the home if you are not feeling well. If you are already traveling and get sick on the flight or cruise, inform the crew members immediately. Contact your doctor as soon as you get home.

What not to do?

Don't touch your face

Don't touch your mouth, eyes, and nose if your hands are not washed. Unwashed hands might have virus that can enter your body when you touch the face.

Don't panic.

Don't panic, according to public health officials the risk of coronavirus is still not that high. Prepare yourself and take proper precautions to save yourself from the attack. Not having or renewing your health insurance can be big mistake.

Avoid contact with animals

Avoid going outside unnecessarily. Stay home and healthy. Avoid contact with animals.
